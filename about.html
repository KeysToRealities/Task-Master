About Task Master

Task Master is a simple productivity app for planning your day, building daily habits, and staying focused.

Features

  • Daily Checklist — track recurring habits, reset each day
  • Calendar — plan tasks on specific dates
  • Focus Timer — run timed focus sessions
  • Feedback — tell us what's working and what's not

Built With

HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, with Firebase for accounts and data.
